## Why should I use this?

This tone indicator **{frontmatter.tag}** is used to clarify that a message is a reference to something. It's especially helpful if the message may be confusing, rude, or have a negative connotation out of context.

<br />

## Examples within messages

- Freelancers deserve to die! {frontmatter.tag}
